Chess

Classical

  • A team consists of 4 players who actually play in tournament and one extra. All the team members must belong to the same Institute.
  • The game is going to be played between 2 players from different teams.
  • If a player from Team A wins against a player from Team B, then he gets 1 point for his team and if they end in a draw, both the players get 0.5 points for their team, to be added into the round points of the team.
  • At the end of each round, the winner team of each match will be given 1 point while the other will be awarded a zero. The winner will be decided on the basis of the round points.
  • If the players of both the teams win 2 matches then in that case there will be a blitz match (5 minutes) between 3 players from each team and the winning team will be decided on the number of matches its players have won.
  • Two illegal moves are allowed in classical.
  • Matches will be of 1 hour 30 min (each player).
  • A separate classical tournament will be held for girls
  • The Arbiter's decision shall be final in case of any discrepancies.

Blitz

  • It will be a singles knockout tournament.
  • Illegal moves are not allowed (loss of first illegal move).
  • Matches will be of 5 min (each player).
  • The Arbiter's decision shall be final in case of any discrepancies.
